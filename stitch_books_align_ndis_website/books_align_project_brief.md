# Books Align Website Design Requirements

## Brand Overview
- **Business:** Books Align, an accounting firm based in Nepal.
- **Specialization:** Outsourced accounting for the Australian disability healthcare (NDIS) sector.
- **Brand Feel:** Warm, welcoming, trustworthy, not corporate or cold.
- **Target Audience:** Disability support providers, NDIS businesses, people with disabilities, and carers.

## Visual Identity
- **Color Palette:** Soft blues and greens (primary), white backgrounds, warm accents (light teal, sage green, sky blue). No harsh dark tones.
- **Typography:** Clean, highly readable sans-serif. Large font sizes, generous line spacing. WCAG 2.1 AA compliance is critical.
- **Visual Style:** Human and grounded photography. Diverse and inclusive imagery featuring people with disabilities and carers. No "stock-photo-cliché" imagery.

## Site Structure & Content

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero:** Strong headline (e.g., "Accounting built for NDIS providers"). Subheading about Nepal-Australia connection.
- **Trust Signals:** Experience, clients served, certifications.
- **Services Overview:** High-level list.
- **"Why Books Align" Section:** Value proposition.
- **CTA:** Contact or book a call.

### 2. Services Page
- **Detailed Sections:** Bookkeeping, BAS/Tax, NDIS Plan & Budget Management, Payroll, Financial Reporting.
- **Layout:** Icon, short description, and "Get started" CTA for each.

### 3. Why Us Page
- **Value Prop:** Remote/Nepal benefits (cost-effective, skilled, dedicated).
- **Expertise:** NDIS sector knowledge and accessibility values.
- **Trust Builders:** Qualifications, software tools (Xero, MYOB), and testimonials.

### 4. Contact Page
- **Form:** Name, business name, email, phone, service, message.
- **Info:** Business email, response time promise.
- **Scheduling:** Calendly embed placeholder.

## Navigation & Footer
- **Navigation:** Sticky top nav. Logo (left), links (center/right), "Client Login" and "Book a Call" buttons.
- **Footer:** Page links, contact info, ABN placeholder, social icons, accessibility statement link.

## Accessibility Requirements
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliant.
- High contrast text.
- Large tap targets.
- Clear focus states.
- Skip navigation.
- Alt text placeholders.

## Competitor Reference
- **arisgroup.com.au:** Match the professionalism and depth, but with a warmer, NDIS-specific feel.
